What is Africa in Tech

Africa in Tech is a community of African-American working in the Tech in Silicon Valley.
The idea behind is to inspire, to provide networking, mentorship and opportunities.

To achieve these goals, we plan to:

  • - Organize monthly (last Friday of the month) meetup @ 42 Silicon Valley by :
  • - Create an online open source plateform and invite developers and non - tech persons around the world to come up and to work together to build social solutions for Africa
  • - Host an international summit and networking between African Tech companies and American companies.
  • - Organize hackathons
